06. Notes

Why is WhatsApp automation necessary?

Immediate response is the highest converting factor in travel sales. Automation resolves client requests, sends itinerary files, and answers FAQs 24/7, preventing potential clients from moving to a competitor.

Why choose custom code (Next.js/Sanity) over site builders?

Load performance. Travel portals depend on large media files. Site builders slow down significantly with heavy photos, resulting in high exit rates. Custom applications load instantly and can be updated easily through a tailored dashboard.

What is the typical development timeline?

Integration of WhatsApp systems, local SEO strategies, or brochure designs requires 2 to 5 days. Full custom web application development takes 3 to 6 weeks.

How are retainers structured?

One-off budgets are applied to web design or setup projects. Ongoing optimization campaigns, local SEO monitoring, and ad accounts are handled under monthly performance retainers.
